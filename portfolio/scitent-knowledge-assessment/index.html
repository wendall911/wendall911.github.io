Assessment delivery system migration to scalable cloud architecture.

Partnership & Architecture

We partnered with Scitent, a prominent eLearning company and built an innovative solution to migrate an existing assessment delivery system to a scalable cloud architecture. The existing infrastructure needed to be updated to enable scalability and flexibility to support their wide range of products and services. Our primary role was to design and implement a complex backend API, user interface requirements and migration infrastructure. While working with Scitent, we were key in adding staff positions to work as direct hires within the organization. We were directly responsible for training and managing this staff for the initial switch to the new technology stack.
